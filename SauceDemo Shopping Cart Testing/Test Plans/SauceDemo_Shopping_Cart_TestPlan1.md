Objective
Verify that users can successfully add, remove, and manage products in the shopping cart, confirm that cart information is accurately maintained throughout the shopping session, and validate related user interface functionality such as cart badge updates and product sorting.

Scope
In Scope: Add products to the shopping cart, Remove products from the shopping cart, Cart badge count updates, Multiple product management, Cart persistence while navigating between pages, Product information displayed in the cart, Checkout button visibility, Product sorting using the filter dropdown

Out of Scope: User authentication, Checkout process, Payment processing, Order confirmation, Performance testing, Security testing, Cross-browser compatibility, Mobile responsiveness

Test Environment
Operating System: Windows 11 Pro
Browser: Google Chrome Version 149.0.7827.156 (64-bit)
Application: Sauce Demo
